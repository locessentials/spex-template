# Unit 2: Infrastructure

Before undertaking translation and localization, certain infrastructure must already be in place. This includes the deep market awareness that will allow you to design products appropriately, definitions around confidentiality and credits, data security built into the systems that make up an organization's infrastructure, and risk management practiced consistently within organizational groups.

# Unit 1: Product Line

The product line parameter group presents key information about the product line to be translated and localized. It broadly identifies the requestor, their verticals, and the clientele who will use the work. This section also collects contact information for production stakeholders to facilitate communication.

Remember, this first parameter group is one of many under the umbrella of product line specifications. The additional product-line parameters groups that you'll work with in these specifications include:

- [Foundational Infrastructure](../unit2/unit2-infrastructure.md)
- [Language and Design Outcomes](../unit3/unit3-outcomes.md)
- [Process Management](../unit4/unit4-process-mgmt.md)
- [Money Matters and Time Matters](../unit5/unit5-money-time.md)
